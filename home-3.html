About Us

Vestry is a church that believes in Jesus, a church that loves God and people.

Vestry is a contemporary Christian church. Overwhelmed by the gift of salvation we have found in Jesus.

We have a heart for authentic worship, are passionate about the local church, and are on mission to see Gods kingdom established across the earth. Vestry Church was founded by Dylan and Stacy Johnes in 1994 in the western suburbs of New York, USA. Now, Vestry has churches in city centres in 23 cities around the country.

We do not seek to reconstruct the church and monastery of St. Catharine, but to preserve it in a safe condition to future generations.

University expenses for former street children

Our specific goal is to improve nutrition and ensure these orphans and vulnerable children attend primary school at Mwererwe Church.
